# Forge Digital — Day One: close a $2k deal

Three channels, ranked by realistic speed. Work them in this order, in parallel where you can. Ahmed works the same list from his side.

## Channel 1 — Warm network (best odds today)

Everyone you and Ahmed know who owns or manages a business, or knows someone who does. Past clients count double. Write the list before you send anything — aim for 30+ names between the two of you.

**WhatsApp/DM script (adjust per person, don't paste blindly):**

> Hey [name], quick one. Ahmed and I just opened our web studio, Forge Digital. We build modern multilingual websites — English plus Arabic or Turkish — for businesses in the US, Canada and Europe. First 3 clients get founding pricing: $2,000 instead of $3,500, delivered in 5 days. Know anyone whose website is old, broken, or missing? Even one intro helps a lot.

Why it works: it asks for an intro, not money — lower pressure, and people say yes to intros. When the intro comes back, you're warm.

**Past clients (SASK, MRT Kuru Buz, Süleyman Seven):** they already trust you. Two asks in one message: (1) can we feature your site in our portfolio, (2) do you know any business owner abroad — relatives, suppliers, friends — who needs a site? Turkish business owners almost always know someone in Germany, the Netherlands, or the UK.

## Channel 2 — Upwork/Fiverr (same-day deals exist here)

This is the only marketplace where strangers do spend $2k on day one, because the platform carries the trust.

- Set up the profile as Forge Digital, lead with the multilingual angle and screenshots of the three sites.
- Search jobs posted in the last 24h: "website for restaurant", "clinic website", "Arabic website", "multilingual website", "small business website".
- Send 10 proposals today. Each one names their business, states one specific thing you'd fix, and offers 5-day delivery. No generic proposals — they're deleted on sight.
- Price at $1,500–2,500. Underpricing signals low quality to Western buyers.

## Channel 3 — Mockup-first cold DM (seeds this week's deals, not today's)

Pick 5 diaspora businesses (Google Maps: Turkish/Arab restaurants, barbershops, clinics in Berlin, London, Toronto, NYC) with bad or missing sites. I generate a homepage mockup for each. You DM/email the owner a screenshot: "We rebuilt your homepage — want the rest?" These convert over days, not hours, but start the clock now.

## The close (any channel)

When someone bites, don't send a proposal document — get them on a 15-minute call today. On the call:

1. Ask what the site needs to do for them (bookings? calls? menu? credibility?).
2. Quote flat: **$2,000, delivered in 5 business days, two revision rounds included.** Optionally $50/mo after month one for hosting + edits.
3. Ask for 50% ($1,000) via Wise/Payoneer to start today, 50% on delivery. A deal isn't closed until the deposit lands.
4. Send a one-paragraph confirmation message restating scope, price, timeline. That's your contract for deal #1.
